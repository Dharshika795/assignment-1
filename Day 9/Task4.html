Border Style

Border styles define how the edges of an element appear. The solid border creates a continuous straight line around the box, giving it a clean and professional look.

The dashed border is made up of short line segments with spaces in between, making the box look decorative or less formal. The double border uses two parallel lines around the element, adding a bold and stylish appearance.

By combining different border styles with padding values, you can control both the inner spacing and the visual emphasis of your design.
