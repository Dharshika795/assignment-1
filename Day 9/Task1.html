Chennai

Chennai is famous as India's "Cultural Capital," known for its rich traditions, historic temples like Kapaleeshwarar Temple, and vibrant classical music and dance forms. It's also a major economic center, particularly for its automobile industry and as a hub for IT services and education, housing institutions like IIT Madras. The city offers unique cultural attractions such as Marina Beach and historical sites reflecting its colonial past.

Thanjavur

Thanjavur is famous for its rich history, culture, and arts, particularly the UNESCO World Heritage Site of the Brihadeeswara Temple.
