How to cook Chicken Biryani

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, chili powder, turmeric, ginger-garlic paste, and salt for 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil and ghee in a pan, add whole spices and sliced onions, and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until it turns soft and releases aroma.
  5. Boil rice until its 70% cooked, then drain the water.
  6. Layer rice and chicken alternately in a pot, sprinkle fried onions and saffron milk on top.
  7. Cover and cook on low flame (dum) for 20 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with raita or boiled egg.
