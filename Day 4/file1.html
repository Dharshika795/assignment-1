Security&Privacy

You can find Instagram's privacy and security settings by going to your profile, tapping the menu icon (three lines) in the top right, and then selecting Settings and activity, followed by either Privacy to manage account visibility and user interactions or Security for password and login information.

  • Private & Public Account.
  • Report and Block unwanted user.
  • Two-Factor Authentication for safety.

Business Tools

In the context of Instagram, "business tools" are features within a business profile that help users understand their performance, promote their content, and engage with customers, including Insights (analytics on followers and posts), Promotion (paid campaigns to boost reach), and a customizable business profile with contact details and shopping features

Instagram provides special tools for businesses and creators:
